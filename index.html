About Me

I am originally from Chicago, but moved to Dallas with my wife in 2011. During my time in Dallas, I learned that I love to create, and one of the most satisfying ways to create things is on a computer! So after working through the front end certification content on freeCodeCamp, I decided that I would want to connect with even more programmers and people learning to program and so I am currently pursuing a fullstack developer position with the different skills I have been gaining.



Throughout this page you will see listings of projects I have worked on and ways to connect with me. I make my code accessible on my gitHub page, and would appreciate any feedback you have for me. Again, please do not hesitate to reach out if you would like to know discuss anything you see on this site, programming in general, or the Chicago Blackhawks.
